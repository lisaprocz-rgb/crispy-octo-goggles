🏡 Disney Vacation Club
MAJOR New DVC Rental Rules in Effect (March 31, 2026)
Disney formalized its Commercial Use Policy for DVC Point Rentals. "Commercial use" triggers include: majority of reservations used by non-Members, booking overlapping reservations, regular advertising of rentals, making more than 20 reservations/year where most aren't used personally. Occasional renters using brokerages like DVC Rental Store are generally unaffected.
