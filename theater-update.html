📰 Broadway News & Buzz
🏆 Tony Awards
79th Annual Tony Awards: June 7, 2026 at Radio City Music Hall
Broadcast live on CBS and streaming on Paramount+, 8–11 PM ET. Returns to Radio City for the first time in years.
🏆 Tony Awards
Nominations Announced May 5 · Eligibility Cutoff April 26
The 2025–26 eligibility window runs April 28, 2025 through April 26, 2026. With the cutoff just days away, the Tony race is in full swing.
🏆 Best Musical Frontrunners
The Lost Boys, TITANÍQUE, and Two Strangers (Carry a Cake Across New York) lead the pack
Three very different musicals generating the most awards heat in the Best Musical category this season.
🏆 Best Revival of a Play
Nathan Lane early favorite for Death of a Salesman; stacked field includes Joe Turner's Come and Gone and Proof
With Ayo Edebiri in Proof and Taraji P. Henson also entering the picture, Best Revival of a Play is one of the most competitive categories of the season.
🏆 Best Revival of a Musical
Cats: The Jellicle Ball is an immediate frontrunner; Ragtime, Chess, and Rocky Horror Show all contending
The drag ballroom reimagining of Cats has generated enormous buzz alongside rarely-seen revivals of Ragtime, Chess, and the incoming Rocky Horror Show.
🎭 Casting
Megan Thee Stallion makes history in Moulin Rouge!
Making her Broadway debut as Zidler (thru May 17), becoming the first female-identifying performer to play the role worldwide. She was briefly hospitalized with exhaustion and dehydration during a performance in April.
🎭 Casting
Adrien Brody and Tessa Thompson make their Broadway debuts in The Fear of 13
The two-time Oscar winner and Golden Globe nominee star in the true-crime drama about Nick Yarris, a man who spent 22 years on death row. Official opening is April 15 at the James Earl Jones Theatre.
🎭 Casting
Jeremy Jordan begins as Bobby Darin in Just In Time April 21
Jonathan Groff played his final performance March 29. Matthew Morrison covered briefly before Jordan takes over at Circle in the Square.
🎭 Casting
J. Harrison Ghee leads new Hadestown principal cast as Hermes
Alongside Joshua Colley (Orpheus), Jordan Tyson (Eurydice), Gary Dourdan (Hades, Broadway debut), and Gaby Moreno (Persephone, Broadway debut).
🎭 Casting
Keri René Fuller takes over as Elphaba in Wicked
Fuller assumed the role full time beginning March 3. Emma Flynn (Cher in West End's Clueless) is making her Broadway debut as Glinda.
🎭 Buzz
Broadway Spring 2026 brings record-setting wave of star power
Nathan Lane, Laurie Metcalf, Ayo Edebiri, Don Cheadle, Kelli O'Hara, Rose Byrne, Taraji P. Henson, Adrien Brody, and Tessa Thompson, among others.

🌱 Industry News
Broadway union contracts ratified through 2028 — strike averted
After tense negotiations in fall 2025, both AFM Local 802 (musicians) and Actors' Equity reached agreements with the Broadway League. What would have been Broadway's first strike since 2007 was averted.
🌱 Broadway Buzz
Inaugural Broadway Ensemble Awards held April 6, 2026
The first-ever Broadway Ensemble Awards celebrated unsung heroes of the stage. Chess and Ragtime were among the big winners of the inaugural ceremony.
