🏆 Tony Awards
Best Musical Race: Schmigadoon! Is the Frontrunner
Schmigadoon! leads early predictions for Best Musical. Cats: The Jellicle Ball (opened Apr 7 as a drag ball extravaganza), The Rocky Horror Show, and The Lost Boys are expected nominees. Strong competition all around.
🏆 Tony Awards
Best Revival Race: Ragtime vs. Chess
Critics favor Ragtime for emotional depth; Chess (Imperial Theatre) is the commercial powerhouse. Death of a Salesman (Nathan Lane & Laurie Metcalf) is a leading Best Play Revival contender. Waiting for Godot also in the mix.
