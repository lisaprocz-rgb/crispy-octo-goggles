Key Races to Watch

PA-01 Congressional Primary (Harvie vs. Simonelli):

The DCCC has placed PA-01 on its national target list. Harvie holds broad establishment support, while Simonelli offers a progressive alternative. Fitzpatrick's considerable cash advantage makes the general election a heavy lift regardless of which Democrat prevails. The Philadelphia Inquirer's March 29 profile positioned Harvie as the party's best shot at flipping this seat.

SD-16 Democratic Primary (Pinsley vs. Merkl-Gump vs. Codner):

The only contested Democratic state legislative primary in the Bucks County area. The winner will face Republican incumbent Jarrett Coleman in November. This race is critical to the broader effort to flip control of the PA State Senate.

HD-142 (Egan vs. Hogan) — US Route 1 Controversy:

Kristin Egan's opposition to the $227M PennDOT US-1 project has given her campaign a defining local issue. This is generating significant grassroots energy in Lower Bucks County and could help her in the general election against incumbent Joe Hogan.

HD-144 (Munroe — Swing District):

Munroe's 144th District remains one of the most closely watched swing seats in the state House. He won by just 515 votes in 2022 and expanded his margin to 1,065 in 2024. Republicans will target this seat aggressively in November.
